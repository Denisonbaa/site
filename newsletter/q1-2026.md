## From the Editor

Welcome to the first issue of the BAA Denison Newsletter.

This is something we've been building toward for a while — a publication that is ours. Not filtered through the university's communications office, not squeezed into a corner of the alumni magazine. Ours. Written by Black Denisonians, edited by Black Denisonians, and built to serve the people in this network who have been waiting for something like this.

Here's what you'll find in this issue:

- A report on the **BAA Scholarship Fund** — where we are, where we're going, and how you can help
- An introduction to our new **Editorial Committee** and how to get involved in future issues
- A reflection from an alumna who reconnected with the BAA after thirty years away
- Updates on **upcoming events** and committee news

We publish quarterly. This is Vol. 1, No. 1. We're glad you're here.

---

## Scholarship Fund Update

The BAA Scholarship Fund is officially live through our Donor-Advised Fund. As of this issue, we have raised **$3,500** toward our **$15,000 goal** for the Spring 2027 award cycle.

Every dollar in this fund goes directly to a Black Denisonian student. We control the fund. We set the criteria. We make the award. That independence is the point.

**How to give:** Email us at [baaDenison@denison.edu](mailto:baaDenison@denison.edu?subject=Scholarship%20Gift) and we'll send you contribution instructions. Gifts of any size are welcome and tax-deductible.

## Welcome to the Editorial Committee

The newsletter you're reading was built by a team of volunteers who gave their time to make something worth reading. We are actively looking for more contributors — writers, editors, photographers, and people who just have something to say.

If you've ever thought *someone should write about this*, that someone can be you. Reach out at [baaDenison@denison.edu](mailto:baaDenison@denison.edu?subject=Newsletter%20Contributor).
