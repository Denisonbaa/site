## From the President

Welcome to the first issue of the BAA Denison Newsletter hosted on the BAA's dedicated website. 

This publication is part of the BAA's journey of infrastructure building, it's an element of how we evolve from a loosely organized affinity group to a fully functional institution in our own right, able to advance goals and aims of the association on multiple fronts from student success to alumni philanthropy, member networking and alumni association thought leadership

Here's what you'll find in this issue:

* A report on the **BAA Endowment** — where we are, where we're going, and how you can help
* A preview of the BAA Capital Collective strategy for alumni philanthropy
* A high level overview of how the BAA can implement an alumni/student mentorship effort
* Details on the Final Exam Care Package Drive 
* Updates on **upcoming events** and committee activity

We publish quarterly. This is Vol. 1, No. 1. We're glad you're here.
